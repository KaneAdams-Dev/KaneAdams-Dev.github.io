Art is Opinion

The art in the gallery you work in has been stolen and you have to replace it. Create portraits, landscapes and still-lifes by drawing what you see or going freestyle using only your eyes and the spacebar. Once completed you can view your masterpieces in the gallery.

Undergraduate group assigment for Experimental Games module using Tobii eye-tracking to create a game, receiving a First. Later continued further into self-directed placement under team name Coloured Perception.

I was the gameplay programmer with main responsibilities the customisation of painting tools, character dialogue, and saving system for art created.
