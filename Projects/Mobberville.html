Mobberville

Help the mobs rebuild their homes in Mobberville. Farm, Cook, Mine, Build and Explore to discover the secrets of the Temple of Baktis!

Masters Summer Self-directed Placement where we were tasked to createa vertical slice for an Unreal game within 8 weeks and advertise it.

I was the Lead gameplay programmer with main responsibilities of creating the player system and core gameplay loop of farming crops and cooking what was harvested.
