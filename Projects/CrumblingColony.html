Crumbling Colony

Search around the kitchen and guide your ants towards biscuits to bring back to the colony. But remember: Strength in Numbers... and watch out for danger!

Crumbling Colony was created in a week for the Brackeys Game Jam 2025.2, following the theme "Risk it for the Biscuit". For this theme, we created an ant strategy game where you direct ants around a kitchen to collect food (specifically biscuits) to bring back home, allowing the ant colony to grow.

As an extra challenge, my partner and I swapped roles and so I spent the week learning blender to create the 3D assets to be used for the game.
