## About the Site
This portfolio site serves as a central hub for my development work, with a focus on:
- Game Development (Unity, C#, gameplay systems)
- Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
- Interactive design and technical problem-solving

The site is designed as an interactive extension of a resume, providing context and reasoning behind the creation of projects and design decisions. 
It applies my game development foundation to the creation of an interactive and intuitive web-based system.

### Project Goals
- Present projects in a clear, scannable format
- Bridge my game development background with web development
- Act as a portfolio piece in itself

#### Why 12 Projects?

The homepage is built around a responsive grid system that adapts across screen sizes:
- 4 columns on large displays
- 3 columns on tablets
- 2 columns on small screens
- 1 column on mobile

A limit of 12 projects was chosen because it allows the grid to remain visually consistent at every breakpoint:
- 3 rows of 4 projects
- 4 rows of 3 projects
- 6 rows of 2 projects
- 12 rows of 1 project

This avoids uneven rows or incomplete columns when resizing the page, ensuring the layout feels intentional and balanced across all devices.

> While fewer projects may be shown during active development, the layout is designed to scale cleanly up to the 12-project limit. <!-- REMOVE WHEN AT 12 GAMES! -->
